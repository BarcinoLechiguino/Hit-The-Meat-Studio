HIT THE MEAT STUDIO

Our Goal

To replicate SEGA's classic fighting arcade game from 1992, featuring one of its characters BLANKA, and release it as a tribute.

About the Game

One of the most well-known fighting games of all time Street Fighter II: The World Warrior. In this tribute you will be able to use one of its's main characters BLANKA, with all his moveset and fighting against a friend in Local-Multiplayer (2 PLAYERS ONLY).
